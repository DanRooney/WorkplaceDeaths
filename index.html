Death in the Workplace

Fatalities in OSHA approved workplaces week by week from Oct. 2015 to Sept. 2016
Data provided by the Occupational Safety and Health Administration shows that of the 1,000+ deaths that occur in OSHA-approved workplaces each year, falling and getting crushed are the top two offenders.
